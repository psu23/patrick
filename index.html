About Me

I am a Full-Stack Web Developer in Philadelphia, PA driven by discovery and innovation.


My name is Patrick and I am a Full-Stack Web Developer living in Philadlephia, PA. I grew up in the suburbs of NYC, and got my BS in Materials Science and Engineering (MSE) from Rutgers University-New Brunswick. I then went to Drexel University, where I got my PhD in MSE. During this time, I discovered several new nanomaterials that have potential applications in quantum computers among others.

During my experiences in academia and industry, I was exposed to coding languages including C++ and Matlab. Using these languages to answer puzzling questions taught me about the powers of coding. My creative background and my interest in design and UX/UI, however, fueled my passion for web development. Creating a better user experience, innovation and problem-solving are my motivations.

Some of my other interests and hobbies are DJ-ing, music production, woodworking, and film history and production.
